What we do

We take an Ecosystem Approach to focus on Skill Development for Employment & Income Generation for low resource communities

We work with youth in low income communities and their ecosystem to empower with the skills, confidence and mindset required for modern-day work environments.

ETASHA’s programmes are focussed on different segments of the community including adolescents, teachers, women, families, school systems, other Vocational Training Providers and the community at large. We don’t just work on skill-development, but on bringing about a positive mindset change in the individual and in the community for sustained employment and income generation.

Vocational Training and Placement

connecting youth with opportunities

Our Experiential, Interactive and Participative methodology of training classes of up to 20 trainees allows for intensive engagement, confidence building, skill development as well as attitude change while increasing knowledge and exposure to the realities of work. In 3-4 months, youth get ready to start on growth-oriented careers, placement for which follows immediately after training.

COUNSELLING and Guidance in Schools

ENGAGING ADOLESCENTS FOR PURPOSEFUL LIVING

Progressively intensive involvement of adolescents in the development of their skills and personal efficacy is crucial to growing into healthy and confident adults. ETASHA guides school-going adolescents in their search for productive and satisfying careers, giving them access to skilled counsellors to help them with their adjustment issues and concerns, this program is geared to building a sound base for a good future.

KNOW MORE

EMPLOYABILITY SKILLS TRAINING & PLACEMENT FOR ITI STUDENTS

Reducing the employability skills gap

Adding comprehensive employability skills and meaningful industry exposure to youth trained in Government approved technical skills reduces the skill mismatch with industry’s requirements, making the youth employment ready. Placement in relevant industrial establishments is facilitated immediately on completion of the programme.

KNOW MORE

TRAINING OF EMPLOYABILITY SKILLS TRAINERS

mentoring mentors

In today’s ever-changing and increasingly competitive environment, every technical trainer and trade instructor needs to work on the holistic development of trainees if the aim is to prepare them for modern industry. This program aims to add mentoring skills and effective training methodologies for skill development of trainees.

Development of Women Entrepreneurs

co-creating economic progress

Assisting, handholding and enabling women to traverse the journey from being diffident, dependent home-makers to becoming self-confident earners, co-owners and managers of successful enterprises is ETASHA’s newest and eminently successful venture.

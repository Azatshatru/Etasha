VOCATIONAL TRAINING AND PLACEMENT

connecting youth with opportunities

Our specialised methodology helps prepare 12th pass girls and boys from low resource settings for the modern-day work environments in short-duration industry-relevant skills training and placement programmes.

ETASHA’s experiential, participative and interactive learning sessions held inside classrooms as well as work-relevant locations all around the city, help bring about a positive mindset change towards one’s own development and future, as well as towards work, employment and performance.

‘Confidence in Computers and Customer Service (CCCS)’ module is designed to prepare trainees for careers like data entry operators, receptionists, office assistants, and other clerical and office-based jobs. The program intake is strictly limited to one student per computer to maximize learning and retain the interest of the students.‘Confidence in Accounts, Tally and Taxation (CATT)’ module prepares the trainees with commerce background for accounts related jobs.

On completion of training, the trainees are assisted with placement at appropriate jobs in established companies after due diligence of the working environment and as per sector and location preference of the trainees. Consequently, the placement rate of our VTP programs has consistently exceeded 65%.

The programme has had a very positive influence on the girls of the community, and their enrolment has increased from an average of 29% during 2006- 2010), to over 60% (2015 onwards)

Current Project Locations: Madanpur Khadar, Tigri, Mangolpuri, Karol Bagh and Begumpur in Delhi, Noida in Uttar Pradesh and Janouli in Haryana.

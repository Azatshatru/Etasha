DEVELOPMENT OF WOMEN ENTREPRENEURS

Co-Creating Economic Progress

ETASHA works in low income communities to create awareness among stay-at-home women with low education and confidence, about the possibilities of becoming successful entrepreneurs. We mobilise groups of women with the desire and need for income generation, train them in basic life skills, followed by enterprise management skills as well as specialised skill training.

ETASHA creates an enabling environment for entrepreneurship development amongst women, facilitates setting up of enterprises by groups of women, and handholds them in running these enterprises including connecting with the suppliers and sales channels till they become quality-oriented, profitable and self-sustaining.

Project Locations: Mangolpuri, Delhi and Janouli, Haryana.
